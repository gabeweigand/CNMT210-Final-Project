Forza: Horizon 4

This game was released on October 2, 2018. I recently bought this game and I have pretty much played through it. But I can’t stop coming back just to drive around and compete in their unique seasonal challenges or to just drive along the gorgeous backdrop of England. This has been one of the most popular car racing games and it easy to see why. Forza Horizon 4 is an incredible game to play for anyone at any level. The races range from street racing to off roading. The have incredible tasks to do outside of racing like stunt driving, drift zones, speed traps, and my personal favorite danger signs. The most unique thing about this game compared to its predecessors is how the seasons change. Every week the game changes and with the seasonal change how you drive, the road conditions, and the types of events available also change. This feature keeps making me come back week after week because it’s like playing an entirely different game week to week.

Listed below are some of my personal reasons as to why this game is a must to play:

  • Two words - Barn Finds! I smile every time a purple circle appears on my map. This means that I get to hunt around for a barn that hold a unique car that you cannot buy from their store of cars. These cars are not only fun to hunt down but also to race in.
  • The expansion packs so far only one expansion has been released- Fortune Island- but with these expansions it unlocks more races and more of the map. It prolongs the joy of the game and with the seasonal changes the expansion is like never before.
  • I absolutely love the wheelspins in this game. Every time you level up or gain influence you work your way towards regular and super wheelspins. These spins are like a slot machine, but you are guaranteed to at least win something- cash/credits, a cool new vehicle, unique character clothing or a new horn can be unlocked. These wheelspins are not only fun but they can be a quick way to expand your car collection or increase your credits to purchase other items
  • The last thing I felt needed to be included is that this game is the first Forza game where you can purchase reality. You can buy houses all around your map and some houses come with super cool bonuses like unlocking skill songs and getting special vehicles. These houses range in style and utility but they can be quick pit stops on drives for players to make upgrades to their cars without having to go all the way back to the few Forza Horizon Hubs.
